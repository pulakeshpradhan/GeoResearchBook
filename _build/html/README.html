Geography Research for Beginners#

Welcome to Geography Research: From Concept to Conclusion. This book is a complete guide designed to take you through the entire research lifecycle, from your very first idea to the final bibliography.

Why this book?#

Many students know how to use software but struggle with why they are using it or how to present their findings scientifically. This book bridges that gap.

Structure#

  1. Part 1: Research Basics - Understanding what research is and how to review literature.

  2. Part 2: Structure - How to write an Introduction, Study Area, and Methodology.

  3. Part 3: Analysis - Using Google Earth Engine to perform actual geospatial analysis.

  4. Part 4: Interpretation - Making sense of your results and writing a discussion.

  5. Part 5: References - Managing citations and avoiding plagiarism.

Author: Pulakesh Pradhan Researcher, Ravenshaw University | Founder, Spatial Geography
